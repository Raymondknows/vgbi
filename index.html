Watch the video to learn more about VIM GLOBAL

VIM GLOBAL seeks to establish bible schools worldwide to train and equip Christians and Pastors to become relevant, functional, and successful in every area of their lives. Founded in 1992 in Ghana, VIM GLOBAL now operates globally with a vision to raise leaders with empowerment to change the world.

With a unique hybrid structure of both in-house and online settings, VIM GLOBAL creates a wholesome learning environment for its students, exposing them to God's highest and best for their lives.
